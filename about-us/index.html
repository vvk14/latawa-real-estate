Where It Began

Our Story

Started with one promise,
kept for ten years

Latawa Real Estate began in a small office in Firozpur with a simple conviction: that buying or selling property shouldn't feel like a gamble. Too many families in Punjab had been let down by unclear paperwork, inflated promises, and brokers who disappeared after the sale.

We set out to do it differently — vetting every listing ourselves, walking clients through every clause, and staying reachable long after the keys change hands. A decade later, that same promise is still what every conversation here starts with.

Today we work across residential, commercial, and investment advisory — but the practice hasn't changed: know the property, know the paperwork, and know the person you're serving.

Our Mission

Make every transaction transparent

To give every client — whether buying their first home or expanding a portfolio — complete clarity on what they're investing in, what it's worth, and what to expect at every step, with no fine print left unexplained.

Our Vision

Punjab's most trusted property name

To be the name families and investors think of first across Punjab — known not for the size of our listings, but for the consistency of doing right by every client, every single time.
